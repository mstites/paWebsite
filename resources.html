Sexual Misconduct & Title IX Resources

Trans at Olin Information

Information for transgender students, particuarly for going through name and email changes.

  • Changing Olin name and email - Rae-Anneapproves name and email changes. You can also reach out to Advafor information. It is a relatively simple one page form to get a name and email change, though there have been issues with people not following through - so make sure to follow up to make sure everyone does their job in getting your info changed.

  • Changing directory photo, pronouns, and/or name - contact Miriam Stulin (Marketing and Communications Specialist) with the updated information. In the past, Rae-Anne and Seth of STAR could also be contacted, but contacting Miriam Stulin directly seems to be the quickest way to get information updated.
